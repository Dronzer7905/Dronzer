# Funding Dronzer

Dronzer is free, open-source software built and maintained by a dedicated community. If Dronzer helps your team or business scale its AI infrastructure, please consider supporting its development.

---

## Ways to Support

### ⭐ Star the Repository

The simplest way to support is to star the repository on GitHub — it helps with visibility and lets us know the project is useful to you.

> [⭐ Star Dronzer on GitHub](https://github.com/dronzer7905/dronzer)

---

- Infrastructure for CI/CD and testing
