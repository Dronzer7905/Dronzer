# Dronzer Governance Model

The Dronzer project operates under a meritocratic governance model. The community is open to everyone, and those who contribute significantly to the project can earn decision-making rights.

## Roles
1. **Users**: Anyone using Dronzer.
2. **Contributors**: Anyone who submits code, docs, or bug reports.
3. **Core Maintainers**: Contributors with commit access and steering committee voting rights.

## Decision Making
Major architectural changes (like those introduced in v2.0) require an RFC (Request for Comments) issue, followed by a consensus vote among Core Maintainers.
