# Support

## Community Support

For free, community-driven support, please use the following channels:

| Channel | Best For |
|---|---|
| [**GitHub Discussions**](https://github.com/dronzer7905/dronzer/discussions) | Architecture questions, general Q&A, use-case advice, show & tell |
| [**GitHub Issues**](https://github.com/dronzer7905/dronzer/issues) | Verified bug reports and concrete feature requests |
| [**Security Advisories**](https://github.com/dronzer7905/dronzer/security/advisories/new) | Privately reporting security vulnerabilities |

### Before Opening an Issue

Please search [existing issues](https://github.com/dronzer7905/dronzer/issues) and [discussions](https://github.com/dronzer7905/dronzer/discussions) first — your question may already be answered.

---

## Enterprise Support

Dronzer offers paid Enterprise Support SLAs for mission-critical deployments. Enterprise features include:

- **24/7 dedicated support channels**
- **Guaranteed 1-hour response time** for Severity 1 (production down) incidents
- **Architecture review and deployment assistance** for on-premises and cloud environments
- **Custom SLA agreements** tailored to your organization's compliance requirements

> 📬 To inquire about Enterprise Support, open a [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/dronzer7905/dronzer/discussions) with the `enterprise` label, or reach out via the contact details on the [GitHub profile](https://github.com/dronzer7905).
